AI-Powered Features
Intelligence baked into
every workflow.
Not just automation. Actual intelligence that understands South African rental law, supplier proximity, and lease compliance.

AI Lease Generation

Chat with Claude AI to generate a fully compliant Rental Housing Act lease. Merge fields, signature blocks, and clause library built in.

Document Intelligence

Upload an existing PDF or DOCX lease. AI extracts tenant names, dates, rent amounts, and every clause — automatically.

Supplier Matching

5-factor algorithm ranks suppliers by proximity, trade skills, price history, owner preference, and rating. Best match, every time.

Native E-Signing

No DocuSign subscription needed. Tenants sign from any device via a secure link. Track completion in real-time with WebSocket updates.

Real-Time Tenant Chat

WebSocket-powered messaging between tenants, agents, and suppliers. Photo attachments, typing indicators, and full audit trail.

Owner Dashboard

Property owners see occupancy rates, income tracking, maintenance status, and lease pipeline — real-time, read-only, and beautiful.
