# Lease Renewal — What You Need to Know

## When should I expect a renewal offer?
Your managing agent should approach you about renewal at least 30 days before your lease expires (and ideally 60–90 days before for longer leases). Check your notice period clause — it is usually 1 to 2 months.

## What happens if I do nothing?
If your fixed-term lease expires without a renewal being signed, your tenancy typically converts to a month-to-month arrangement under the same terms unless the landlord has given you written notice to vacate. A month-to-month tenancy continues indefinitely until one party gives notice equal to the notice period in your lease.

## Can the landlord increase my rent at renewal?
Yes. A rent increase is negotiated at renewal. In South Africa there is no legislated cap on private residential rent increases, but the Rental Housing Tribunal can rule on increases that are grossly unreasonable. The increase must be communicated in writing before the renewal date.

## What if I want to renew but the landlord doesn't?
The landlord can choose not to renew at the end of a fixed-term lease. They must give you written notice equal to your notice period (as specified in your lease). You cannot be evicted without a valid court order regardless of the lease term.

## How do I renew on Klikk?
Your agent will send you a renewal notice through the portal. Once new terms are agreed, a new lease document will be generated and sent for digital signature. You will receive an email and an in-app notification.

## What if I want to renew but on different terms?
You can counter-propose different terms (rent amount, lease length). Communicate this to your agent in writing as early as possible.

## Early termination during renewal negotiations
If you are still in a fixed-term lease, you cannot leave before the end date without triggering the early termination clause (see your lease — typically 1–2 months' penalty rent). A month-to-month tenancy requires notice equal to your notice period.

## RHA protections at renewal
Under the Rental Housing Act, any renewal must be concluded in writing and both parties must receive a copy. Verbal agreements for lease renewals are not enforceable.
