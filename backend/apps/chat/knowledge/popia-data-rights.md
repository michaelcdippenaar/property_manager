# Your POPIA Data Rights as a Tenant

South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) gives you specific rights over the personal information Klikk and your managing agent hold about you.

## What personal information is held about me?
This typically includes:
- Your full name, ID number, contact details
- Lease agreement and rental history
- Payment records
- Maintenance requests you have raised
- Chat messages (stored for 90 days, then deleted)
- Any documents you uploaded (ID copy, proof of income, etc.)

## Your rights under POPIA

**Right to access (s23)**
You can request a copy of all personal information held about you. Submit a request through your managing agent or via the Klikk portal.

**Right to correction (s24)**
If any information is incorrect, you can request that it be corrected. For example, if your ID number or address is wrong in the system, contact your agent.

**Right to object (s11)**
You can object to the processing of your personal information for purposes beyond your tenancy, such as marketing communications.

**Right to deletion**
Once your lease ends and the legally required retention period has passed, you can request that your personal information be deleted. Note: some information must be kept for legal and accounting reasons (SARS compliance, RHA obligations).

## AI chat messages and POPIA
Messages you send through the Klikk AI chat are:
- Processed by an AI service (Anthropic) to generate replies.
- Stored for 90 days for support purposes, then automatically deleted.
- PII-scrubbed before being sent to the AI (your SA ID number and bank account numbers are masked).

You were notified of this processing before your first chat message.

## Cross-border transfers
The AI service used by Klikk (Anthropic) is based in the United States. This constitutes a cross-border transfer of personal information under POPIA s72. Anthropic processes your messages only for AI inference and does not retain or re-use them for training.

## How to exercise your rights
Contact your managing agent in writing, or submit a data request through the Klikk portal under Settings > Privacy. Klikk must respond to access or correction requests within a reasonable time (generally 30 days).

## Information Regulator
If you believe your rights have been violated, you can lodge a complaint with the South Africa Information Regulator:
- Website: inforegulator.org.za
- Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za
