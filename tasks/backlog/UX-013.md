## Goal
Rewrite the tenant invite and welcome emails with enough context that a first-time recipient understands what Klikk is, what to do, and when the link expires — reducing invite abandonment and confusion.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] `invite_tenant.md` body: 3–4 sentences explaining Klikk in plain language, what happens when the link is clicked, and explicit "This link expires in 72 hours" statement in the email body (not just frontmatter)
- [ ] `welcome_tenant.md` CTA updated from "View your lease" to "Go to your portal" (lease may not yet be signed at send time)
- [ ] Both emails pass a Flesch-Kincaid Grade 8 or below readability check (tool or manual check acceptable)
- [ ] Copy reviewed by GTM before merge
- [ ] No HTML/template rendering broken (test both emails send via `mcp__tremly-e2e__send_test_email` or equivalent)

## Files likely touched
- `content/emails/invite_tenant.md`
- `content/emails/welcome_tenant.md`

## Test plan
**Manual:**
- Trigger an invite send for a test tenant; confirm new copy renders correctly in inbox (Gmail + Apple Mail)
- Confirm 72h expiry copy is visible without opening frontmatter
- Confirm welcome email CTA is "Go to your portal"

**Automated:**
- Readability check script or manual FK score calculation
