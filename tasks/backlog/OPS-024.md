## Goal

Engage a South African attorney to review and sign off on the four template-based legal pages published at launch (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Cookie Policy, Acceptable Use Policy), then remove the "Pending attorney review" footer disclaimer from all four pages.

## Context

At launch, OPS-004 published template-based legal pages with a "Pending attorney review" footer disclaimer per DEC-022 (MC, 2026-04-24). This task completes the attorney engagement loop and removes the disclaimer once sign-off is obtained.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] SA attorney engaged and briefed on all four documents: Privacy Policy (`content/legal/privacy-policy.md`), Terms of Service (`content/legal/terms-of-service.md`), Cookie Policy (`content/legal/cookie-policy.md`), Acceptable Use Policy (`content/legal/acceptable-use.md`)
- [ ] Attorney feedback incorporated — each document amended as needed; changes committed to `content/legal/` and bumped to a new version in the `LegalDocument` backend registry (`requires_re_ack: true` if material changes)
- [ ] "Pending attorney review" disclaimer removed from the footer of all four Astro pages (`website/src/pages/legal/`)
- [ ] Website rebuilt and deployed; legal pages verified live without the disclaimer
- [ ] `LegalDocument` version records updated with `attorney_reviewed: true` (or equivalent metadata field — add the field if it does not exist)
- [ ] Existing users with accepted consent records are prompted to re-accept if any document was materially changed (requires_re_ack flag triggers re-acceptance on next login)
