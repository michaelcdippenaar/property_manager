## Goal
Surface a "How to pay rent" section in the tenant Lease tab showing banking details, payment reference, and due date so tenants never need to call the agent to find out where to pay.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Tenant Lease tab shows a "How to pay" section with: bank name, account number, branch code, account holder, and unique payment reference
- [ ] Payment reference is displayed in a copyable monospace field (tap-to-copy on mobile)
- [ ] Due date is surfaced alongside the payment section (re-use existing field if already shown)
- [ ] Section includes a plain-language note on the consequence of late payment per RHA (e.g. "Payment received after the [due day] of the month may be subject to a late penalty as per your lease agreement")
- [ ] Tenant serializer/endpoint exposes payment details scoped to the authenticated tenant's lease only — no cross-tenant data leakage
- [ ] `BankAccount` is resolved from the lease's property/landlord and serialized; `payment_reference` from `Lease.payment_reference`
- [ ] Section hidden gracefully if no `BankAccount` is on record (with placeholder: "Your agent will provide payment details")

## Files likely touched
- `tenant/src/views/leases/LeaseView.vue` — add "How to pay" section
- `backend/apps/leases/schema.py` — extend tenant lease serializer to include payment details (line ~109)
- `backend/apps/leases/views.py` — tenant lease detail endpoint
- `backend/apps/properties/models.py` — `BankAccount` (lines 222-229)
