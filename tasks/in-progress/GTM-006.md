## Goal
Make every marketing action measurable from impression → click → signup → activation → paid. Without this the campaigns in GTM-003/004/005 are pure guesswork.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Analytics stack chosen and installed:
  - Marketing + product: Plausible (privacy-first, POPIA-friendly, af-south-1 if available) OR PostHog (with cookie-less config)
  - Conversion events: signup_started, signup_completed, first_property_created, first_mandate_signed, first_lease_signed, subscription_started
- [ ] UTM convention documented in `content/marketing/utm-convention.md` (utm_source, medium, campaign, content, term — controlled vocab)
- [ ] Every outbound link in website, emails, sales decks uses correct UTMs (linter / style guide)
- [ ] Attribution: first-touch + last-touch + linear — pick one primary model, compute in analytics
- [ ] Per-channel dashboard: impressions, clicks, signups, activations, CAC, payback (`docs/marketing/attribution-dashboard.md`)
- [ ] Cohort view: week-over-week signup-to-activation funnel
- [ ] Weekly review ritual documented (who looks at what, when)
- [ ] POPIA: consent banner + cookie policy aligned; no PI leaked to third-party analytics
- [ ] Ties into OPS-002 Sentry releases (tag releases with campaign context where relevant)

## Test plan
**Manual:**
- Click a campaign link with UTMs → analytics shows the session with correct params
- Complete a signup flow → all funnel events present in order
- Check POPIA: consent declined → no analytics events fired
