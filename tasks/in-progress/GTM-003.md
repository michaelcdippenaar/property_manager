### 2026-04-22 — rentals-reviewer — review → in-progress (changes requested)

Two defects require fixes before this can proceed to testing. Both are in `content/website/copy/home.md`.

**Defect 1 — Hero stats row: Pro unit cap stated without property cap (line 51)**

`home.md` Section 1 Hero stats row reads:

> `R2,500/mo | For up to 100 units`

`pricing.yaml` Pro tier defines `properties: 50` and `units: 100` with an explicit comment: "50 properties OR 100 units — whichever first." The stat omits the 50-property limit entirely. A boutique agency managing 60 properties but only 60 units reads this stat and believes they fit in Pro; they don't — they've exceeded the property cap. This creates a false expectation at the very first impression point.

Fix: change the stat label to `Up to 50 props / 100 units` or pick a different stat (e.g., the pricing subheadline comparison already handles this correctly at Section 6 — consider replacing with `R12,500+/mo → R2,500/mo` as a savings stat instead).

**Defect 2 — Meta description is 158 characters, acceptance criterion requires ≤155 (line 11)**

`home.md` SEO section meta description:

> "AI-powered lease generation, native e-signing, and a tenant app — built for the Rental Housing Act. Free for your first 5 properties. No credit card required."

Character count: 158 (verified). Acceptance criterion states 155 chars. Trim 3+ characters without losing the substance — e.g. remove "and" before "a tenant app" and trim "No credit card required." to "No card required." (saves 4 chars).
