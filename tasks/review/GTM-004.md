### 2026-04-22 — rentals-reviewer

**Review requested changes.** Two fixes required before this can move to testing.

**1. `content/sales/one-pager.md` — Free tier AI contracts cap is omitted (misleads prospects)**

The pricing table in `one-pager.md` describes the Free tier as "full AI suite (bring your own LLM key)" with no mention of the 2 AI-generated contracts/year cap. `content/product/pricing.yaml` is explicit: `ai_contracts_yearly: 2` on the free tier. A prospect reading the one-pager will reasonably conclude they get unlimited AI leases on free. When they hit the cap after lease 2, that is a trust-eroding surprise.

Fix: add a footnote or inline qualifier to the Free tier row, e.g. "2 AI-generated leases per year (bring your own LLM key)." The Pro tier row should confirm "unlimited AI leases" to make the contrast clear. The current Pro row already says "unlimited AI leases" so only the Free tier row needs the cap language added.

Specifically — `content/sales/one-pager.md`, pricing table, Free tier cell (line 93):
- Current: `R0/month | Up to 5 properties — free forever. Bring your own LLM key.`
- Required: `R0/month | Up to 5 properties — free forever. 2 AI leases/year. Bring your own LLM key.`

**2. `content/sales/demo-script.md` — PayProp objection wording stronger than the guardrail in objections.md**

Scene 4 objection handler (line 228) says:
> "We're building a direct integration."

The objections.md "what not to say" for the same objection reads:
> "Do not promise a timeline on the PayProp integration."

The supporting points section also says:
> "The integration is on the roadmap (not yet built — do not imply it works today)."

"We're building a direct integration" implies active in-progress work and may be heard as a near-term commitment. The safe wording already in `objections.md` is:
> "We're building a direct integration" should be softened to "A direct integration is on our roadmap."

Fix: in `content/sales/demo-script.md` line 228, change:
- Current: `"Great — Klikk complements PayProp. PayProp handles payments; Klikk handles everything before and after the payment — leases, maintenance, tenant comms, owner reporting. We're building a direct integration."`
- Required: replace "We're building a direct integration." with "A direct integration is on our roadmap." (matches the safer phrasing already used in objections.md supporting points)
