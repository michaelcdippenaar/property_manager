**Summary of decisions:**
- Plan is written for a 1–3 person founder-led team; no activities require a marketing hire to execute.
- Direct outreach (warm network, 20–30 contacts) is designated the primary channel for launch — highest conversion potential before social proof exists.
- LinkedIn is the secondary channel; founder's personal account only (not a company page) to maximise organic reach.
- Webinar scheduled for launch week: "AI Lease Generation and the RHA — live demo."
- Facebook group strategy is participation-first, not promotional — targets T2 self-managing landlords.
- PR outreach targets Property Professional, Business Insider SA, Property360, TechCentral, MyBroadband.
- All PLANNED features (screening, inspections, deposit management, multi-portal advertising) are excluded from every campaign asset.
- Budget starts at R0; paid media gated on organic conversion signal in first 2 weeks.
- Dependencies on GTM-003 (homepage) and GTM-004 (sales assets) called out explicitly in both the plan and a dependency table.
- Email sequence copy (GTM-006 scope flag) and blog post content (2 posts outlined but not drafted) are identified as follow-on work items.
