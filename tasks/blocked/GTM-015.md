## Goal
Ensure conversion events from users who navigate directly to `app.klikk.co.za` (skipping the marketing site consent flow) are captured in Plausible, so funnel metrics are not silently understated.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Admin SPA includes its own Plausible script tag gated behind a consent flag (`/api/v1/me/consent` or a first-login consent banner), OR campaign links to `/signup` are routed through the marketing site so consent is captured before redirect
- [ ] Conversion events (`signup_completed`, `first_property_created`, `first_mandate_signed`, `first_lease_signed`, `subscription_started`) appear in Plausible for a test user who navigates directly to `app.klikk.co.za/signup`
- [ ] No duplicate events for users who arrive via the marketing site
- [ ] PM decision on consent UX approach (in-app banner vs. server-side Events API) documented as accepted approach

## Files likely touched
- `admin/src/plugins/plausible.ts`
- `admin/index.html` (script tag injection)
- `backend/apps/accounts/views.py` or signals (if server-side Events API chosen)

## Test plan
**Manual:**
- Open `app.klikk.co.za/signup` directly (no prior visit to `klikk.co.za`), complete signup — confirm `signup_completed` event appears in Plausible dashboard

## Handoff notes
Promoted from discovery `2026-04-22-admin-spa-plausible-direct-traffic-gap.md` (found during GTM-006). Direct-app users are the likely majority of campaign-link signups; their events are currently dropped silently.
