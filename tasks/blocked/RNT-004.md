### 2026-04-22 — implementer

**Code fix:** `dispatch_award` in `backend/apps/maintenance/views.py` now creates a `SupplierJobAssignment` when a job is awarded to a supplier that has a `linked_user` account. The assignment copies the property address and tenant contact name/phone at award time (POPIA data minimisation). If the supplier has no linked user account, the assignment is silently skipped and the award still succeeds. `get_or_create` ensures idempotency.

**Tests added** (all 40 pass, 19 new) in `backend/apps/test_hub/maintenance/integration/test_maintenance.py`:
- `SupplierMatchingTests` (7 tests) — verifies the 5-factor `rank_suppliers` algorithm: preferred supplier ranks first, scores bounded 0–100, non-matching trade scores lower, top-N limit, price history affect, no-geo fallback.
- `SupplierNoLoginPortalTests` (8 tests) — verifies token-based supplier portal: GET marks viewed, POST submits quote, duplicate quote rejected, decline works, closed job blocked, dispatch transitions to quoting, invalid token 404.
- `SupplierJobAssignmentTests` (4 tests) — verifies SupplierJobAssignment is created on award, copies address, skips when no linked_user, idempotent on double-award.
