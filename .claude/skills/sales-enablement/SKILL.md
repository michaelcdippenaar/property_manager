# Sales Enablement

You are supporting the Klikk sales process. Use this skill for call prep, objection handling, demo scripting, and prospect research.

## Before Any Sales Task

1. **Check feature status:** Read `content/product/features.yaml` — never claim a PLANNED feature is available
2. **Know the ICP:** Read `content/sales/icp.md` for target customer profiles
3. **Know objections:** Read `content/sales/objections.md` for prepared responses
4. **Know the demo flow:** Read `content/sales/demo-flow.md` for the structured 20-min walkthrough

## Ideal Customer Profile (Summary)

| Segment | Units | Key Pain | Message |
|---------|-------|----------|---------|
| PM companies | 5-50 | Tool sprawl, manual processes | Replace 6 tools with one AI platform |
| Independent landlords | 1-10 | Lease disputes, midnight calls | Manage like a pro without hiring one |
| Estate agents | 10-100 | Rental book is a cost centre | Turn burden into profit |

**Location:** Gauteng, Western Cape, KZN (primary)
**Disqualifiers:** Commercial-only, international, needs trust accounting as primary use case

## Key Selling Points

1. **Free for 5 units** — removes all risk, no credit card
2. **AI lease generation** — 3 minutes vs hours, RHA-compliant
3. **4 apps** — every person gets their own experience
4. **15-stage lifecycle** — most complete coverage in SA market
5. **Built by a landlord** — not a tech company guessing at problems

## Competitor Positioning

- **vs PayProp:** Complementary, not competitive. Klikk does everything PayProp doesn't.
- **vs WeConnectU:** Similar breadth, but cheaper and AI-native. Their small clients are overpaying.
- **vs Spreadsheets:** "You're already doing the work. Klikk just organises it."

Read `content/brand/competitors.md` for full competitive intel and objection responses.
