# Compliance Documents a Rental Business Must Maintain

## 9. Minimum-viable 12 for a small landlord

If you only build 12, these 12:

1. PAIA/POPIA Manual
2. Privacy Notice (tenant + landlord + supplier — one combined doc)
3. POPIA Master Policy
4. IO Appointment Letter + Deputy
5. RoPA / Data Inventory
6. Retention Schedule
7. Information Security Policy
8. Incident Response Plan + breach templates
9. Operator DPA template + signed DPAs with top 5 vendors
10. Consent Suite (general + credit + marketing + biometric)
11. DSAR Procedure + Forms 1–4 + Register
12. Employee Privacy Notice + Confidentiality

## 10. Document lifecycle

| Phase | Action |
|---|---|
| **Create** | Use Regulator templates; customise for rental context |
| **Approve** | IO or board sign-off |
| **Publish** | Website + head office + lease annexures |
| **Train** | Every staff member annually |
| **Review** | At least annually + on any law change |
| **Retire** | Archive with version history — do not delete (evidentiary) |

## 11. Where to source templates

- **Information Regulator** — `inforegulator.org.za`
- **SAHRC** — PAIA Guide
- **SAICA / LSSA / Law Society** — member templates
- **Werksmans / ENS / Michalsons / Cliffe Dekker Hofmeyr** — published POPIA toolkits
- **Paid tools** — OneTrust, TrustArc (enterprise); Novation POPIAssess (SA SME)

Avoid generic GDPR templates without POPIA-specific adjustments — section references, Regulator contact, and SA-specific rights differ.
