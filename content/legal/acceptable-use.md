**Effective date:** 24 April 2026
**Status:** Pending attorney review — this document is a good-faith draft and will be finalised by our legal counsel.

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules for using the Klikk platform ("Platform"). It applies to all users — property managers, letting agents, landlords, property owners, and anyone else with a Klikk account.

By creating an account or using the Platform, you agree to follow this AUP. Breaching it may result in suspension or permanent termination of your account.

---

## 2. Who this applies to

| User type | Covered by this AUP |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Agency admins | Yes |
| Agents and staff | Yes |
| Property owners | Yes |
| Tenants (where platform access is granted) | Yes |

This AUP applies in addition to the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms). Where there is a conflict, the Terms of Service take precedence.

## 3. Prohibited conduct

You must not use the Platform to:

### 3.1 Illegal activity

- Conduct any activity that is unlawful under South African law, including the **Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999 (RHA)**, the **Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA)**, the **Financial Intelligence Centre Act 38 of 2001 (FICA)**, the **Prevention of Organised Crime Act 121 of 1998 (POCA)**, or any other applicable legislation.
- Facilitate unlawful discrimination in tenant selection on any ground prohibited by the **Constitution of the Republic of South Africa, 1996** or the RHA (section 4(1)(b) specifically prohibits unfair discrimination by landlords).
- Process or store proceeds of crime, engage in money laundering, or circumvent FICA obligations.

### 3.2 Harmful or offensive content

- Upload, share, or transmit content that is defamatory, harassing, threatening, obscene, fraudulent, or that constitutes hate speech as defined in the **Promotion of Equality and Prevention of Unfair Discrimination Act 4 of 2000 (PEPUDA)**.
- Publish false or misleading information about properties, landlords, tenants, or other users.

### 3.3 Spam and unsolicited communications

- Send unsolicited commercial communications ("spam") to tenants, owners, or third parties via the Platform in violation of ECTA s45 or the **Protection of Personal Information Act** consent requirements.
- Harvest contact details from the Platform for use in bulk marketing campaigns without the recipient's consent.

### 3.4 Scraping and automated extraction

- Use automated scripts, bots, crawlers, or other tools to scrape, extract, copy, or harvest data from the Platform without Klikk's prior written consent.
- Export, download, or otherwise extract data at scale for purposes outside your legitimate use of the Platform.
- Use the Platform's API in a manner inconsistent with your subscription tier or these Terms.

### 3.5 Reverse engineering and security

- Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or extract the source code of the Platform or its underlying infrastructure.
- Probe, scan, or test the Platform's security vulnerabilities without Klikk's written authorisation.
- Introduce malware, ransomware, viruses, or any code designed to damage, disable, or gain unauthorised access to the Platform or other users' data.
- Attempt to circumvent authentication, access controls, or rate limits.

### 3.6 Impersonation and identity fraud

- Impersonate any person, entity, or organisation — including Klikk staff, government bodies, or another user.
- Use a false identity to register an account or obtain services.
- Submit false documentation (e.g. forged lease agreements, fabricated identity documents) through the Platform.

### 3.7 Intellectual property infringement

- Upload or transmit material that infringes the copyright, trademark, trade secret, patent, or other intellectual property rights of a third party, in violation of the **Copyright Act 98 of 1978** or the **Trade Marks Act 194 of 1993**.
- Use Klikk's trademarks, logos, or branding without express written permission.

### 3.8 Platform integrity

- Resell, sublicense, or commercially exploit Platform access without Klikk's written consent.
- Use the Platform to operate a competing service or to benchmark Klikk's systems without authorisation.
- Interfere with the availability or performance of the Platform or its infrastructure.

## 4. Enforcement

Klikk reserves the right to investigate suspected AUP violations and to take action proportionate to the severity of the breach.

### 4.1 Graduated response

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Minor** (first offence, unintentional) | Written warning; user advised to correct behaviour |
| **Moderate** (repeated or deliberate) | Temporary suspension of account with minimum 5 business days' notice |
| **Serious** (fraud, illegal content, security attack, data harvesting) | Immediate suspension without notice, pending investigation |
| **Confirmed serious breach** | Permanent termination of account; data retained for legal obligations; referral to law enforcement where required |

### 4.2 Appeals

If your account is suspended, you may appeal within **10 business days** by emailing support@klikk.co.za with the subject line "AUP Appeal — [your account email]". We will review your appeal and respond within 10 business days.

### 4.3 Law enforcement

Klikk will cooperate with South African law enforcement and regulatory bodies (including the South African Police Service, the Information Regulator, and the National Consumer Commission) in accordance with applicable law.

## 5. Your responsibility for third-party content

You are responsible for all content uploaded, transmitted, or shared through your Klikk account, including content uploaded by your agents or employees. Ensure that any personal information you submit about tenants, property owners, or other third parties is collected and shared lawfully under POPIA s11.

## 6. Reporting abuse

If you witness or become aware of a breach of this AUP by another user, please report it to us at **support@klikk.co.za** with as much detail as possible. We treat all reports confidentially.

Reports can also be directed to:
- **Information Regulator (POPIA / data misuse):** [inforeg.org.za](https://inforeg.org.za)
- **South African Police Service (criminal conduct):** [saps.gov.za](https://www.saps.gov.za)
- **National Consumer Commission (CPA violations):** [thencc.org.za](https://www.thencc.org.za)
