## 1. Our commitment

Klikk (Pty) Ltd is committed to the lawful, responsible, and transparent processing of personal information in accordance with the **Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013** ("POPIA"), which came into full effect on 1 July 2021.

---

---

## 3. Operator relationships

Where Klikk processes personal information on behalf of another Responsible Party (for example, a property agency that uses the Klikk platform to manage their clients' data), Klikk acts as an **Operator** as defined in POPIA s1.

In this capacity, Klikk:

- Processes PI only on the documented instructions of the Responsible Party
- Does not retain PI beyond the term of the service agreement
- Provides reasonable technical and organisational security measures
- Assists the Responsible Party in meeting their POPIA obligations
- Enters into a written Operator Agreement with each Responsible Party customer

Where Klikk holds its own direct relationship with a data subject (e.g., platform account holder), Klikk acts as Responsible Party.

---

## 4. The eight conditions for lawful processing

Klikk applies all eight conditions for lawful processing set out in POPIA Chapter 3:

| Condition | Our approach |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Accountability** | Our Information Officer oversees compliance; internal policies and procedures are maintained |
| **Processing limitation** | We collect only PI that is adequate, relevant, and not excessive for the specific purpose |
| **Purpose specification** | Purposes are clearly defined at collection; PI is not processed for incompatible purposes |
| **Further processing limitation** | Further processing is compatible with original purpose or based on new consent |
| **Information quality** | We provide in-app mechanisms to update or correct PI; we keep records current |
| **Openness** | This statement and our Privacy Policy disclose our processing activities |
| **Security safeguards** | Technical and organisational measures as described in our Privacy Policy s9 |
| **Data subject participation** | Data subjects may access, correct, delete, or object to processing of their PI |

---

## 5. Special personal information

POPIA s26–35 affords heightened protection to "special personal information" including health information, religious or political beliefs, trade union membership, criminal records, and financial information.

Klikk does not intentionally collect special personal information except:
- **Financial information** (monthly income, bank details): collected only where necessary for rental applications and disbursements, on a lawful basis.
- **SA ID numbers**: collected for FICA identity verification purposes.

---

## 6. Direct marketing

Klikk only sends direct marketing communications to users who have explicitly opted in. Each marketing communication includes a clear, functional unsubscribe mechanism. We do not sell PI to third-party marketers.

---

## 7. Cross-border transfers

All PI is stored and processed on AWS af-south-1 (Cape Town) — within South Africa. Cross-border transfers are avoided; where unavoidable (e.g., third-party API integrations), Klikk ensures adequate protection as required by POPIA s72 and confirms the destination country has adequate protection or obtains the data subject's consent.

---

## 8. Security incidents

In the event of a security compromise involving PI, Klikk will:

- Notify the Information Regulator as soon as reasonably possible (POPIA s22)
- Notify affected data subjects where there is a real risk of harm
- Document the incident, cause, and remedial actions taken

Security incidents may be reported to: security@klikk.co.za

---

## 9. Data subject rights

Data subjects may exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Access** their PI: submit a PAIA Form 2 request to info-officer@klikk.co.za
- **Correction or deletion**: email privacy@klikk.co.za or use in-app profile settings
- **Object** to processing: email privacy@klikk.co.za with reason
- **Withdraw consent**: unsubscribe link in all marketing emails, or email privacy@klikk.co.za
- **Complain** to the Information Regulator: inforeg.org.za

We respond to data subject requests within **30 days**.
