# Ideal Customer Profiles

## Primary: Small-Medium Property Management Companies

- **Units:** 5-50 residential
- **Location:** Gauteng, Western Cape, or KZN
- **Current tools:** Excel + WhatsApp + paper leases, or basic software they've outgrown
- **Pain points:** Manual lease creation, slow maintenance dispatch, no tenant self-service
- **Decision maker:** Principal or operations manager
- **Budget:** R49/unit/month is less than one hour of admin time saved
- **Message:** "Replace your 6-tool stack with one AI platform"

## Secondary: Independent Landlords (Self-Managing)

- **Units:** 1-10 residential
- **Location:** Any SA metro
- **Current tools:** WhatsApp groups, paper leases, personal spreadsheets
- **Pain points:** Lease disputes from non-compliant agreements, midnight maintenance calls, no paper trail
- **Decision maker:** The landlord (often has a day job)
- **Budget:** Free tier (1-5 units) or R49/unit for 6-10
- **Message:** "Manage like a pro agency without hiring one"

## Tertiary: Estate Agents with Rental Books

- **Units:** 10-100 residential
- **Location:** Established agencies in SA metros
- **Current tools:** Prop Data, WeConnectU, or manual processes
- **Pain points:** Rental book is a cost centre, tenant comms are manual, owner reporting is ad-hoc
- **Decision maker:** Principal agent or rental department head
- **Budget:** R39/unit Agency tier — positioned as cost saving vs current tools
- **Message:** "Turn your rental book from a burden into profit"

## Disqualifiers

- Commercial property only (we're residential-focused)
- International portfolios (SA law-specific)
- Needs trust accounting as primary use case (point them to PayProp, offer integration later)
