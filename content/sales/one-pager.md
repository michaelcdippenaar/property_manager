## The problem

South African rental agents and landlords manage tenants, leases, maintenance, and owner reporting across a patchwork of WhatsApp, Excel, Word, and email. Every gap between those tools is an error waiting to happen — a late lease, a missed maintenance job, an owner complaint.

The incumbent tools that address this (WeConnectU, Prop Data) are expensive, complex, and built for enterprise agencies. Small and mid-size portfolios are overpaying for features they don't use, or still doing it manually.

---

## What Klikk does

Klikk is South Africa's first AI-native rental management platform. One platform. Four apps. Everything from viewing to lease renewal — built for the Rental Housing Act.

---

## Top value propositions

### 1. AI lease generation in minutes, not hours
Chat with Claude AI to generate a fully compliant Rental Housing Act lease. Merge fields, signature blocks, and a reusable clause library are built in. You review and approve every word — AI does the drafting.

### 2. Native e-signing — no DocuSign subscription
Tenants, owners, and agents sign from any device via a 256-bit TLS-encrypted link. No app download required. Track completion in real-time. Fully compliant with ECTA section 13.

### 3. AI maintenance triage and supplier dispatch
Tenants report issues via the mobile app. AI classifies urgency and trade, ranks suppliers on five factors (proximity, skills, price history, owner preference, rating), and dispatches automatically. No phone calls. Full audit trail.

### 4. Tenant mobile app — stop the midnight calls
Flutter-native iOS and Android app. Tenants report issues with photos, chat in real-time, view lease details, and sign documents. A professional tenant experience that reduces inbound calls.

### 5. Owner dashboard — real-time without the monthly report
Property owners see occupancy, income tracking, and maintenance pipeline in real-time. Read-only and self-serve — owners stop calling you for updates.

### 6. Agent mobile app
Native iOS and Android app for rental agents. Manage properties, book and track viewings, capture prospect details, and manage your pipeline from your phone.

### 7. Digital rental mandates
Generate Full Management, Letting Only, Rent Collection, or Finders Fee mandates. Owner and agent sign digitally. The countersigned PDF is stored automatically. Required before any property can be listed or managed.
