## The only AI-native rental management platform built for South African law.

Four purpose-built apps — for agents, tenants, owners, and suppliers — connected by AI that actually does the work.

## Shipped features (all BUILT, in production)

- **AI Lease Generation** — Chat with AI to build a fully compliant RHA lease with merge fields, signature blocks, and your clause library
- **Native E-Signing** — 256-bit TLS-encrypted signing links; real-time WebSocket completion tracking
- **Document Intelligence** — Upload a PDF or DOCX lease; AI extracts every field and clause automatically
- **Rental Mandate E-Signing** — Full Management, Letting Only, Rent Collection, or Finders Fee — digitally signed before the first tenant moves in
- **AI Maintenance Triage** — Classifies urgency and trade; dispatches the right supplier every time
- **5-Factor Supplier Matching** — Proximity, trade skills, price history, owner preference, and rating
- **Tenant Mobile App** — Report issues with photos, sign documents, view lease, chat with property-aware AI (iOS + Android)
- **Agent Mobile App** — Book viewings, capture prospects, manage the pipeline from the field (iOS + Android)
- **Viewing Scheduler** — Schedule and confirm viewings; capture prospect details on the spot
- **AI Tenant Chat** — Property-scoped knowledge base: lease terms, house rules, appliance manuals, maintenance history — tenants get answers without calling the agent
- **Owner Dashboard** — Real-time occupancy, income, and maintenance status
- **Supplier Portal** — Receive job notifications, submit quotes, manage calendar — no login required to quote
- **Rent Payment Tracking** — Unique references, receipts, and automatic arrears flagging
- **Lease Renewal** — Automatic expiry reminders; generate renewal agreements from existing lease data
- **Klikk MCP Server** — Connect Claude or any MCP-compatible AI directly to your portfolio; draft leases, chase tickets, pull stats from a conversation
