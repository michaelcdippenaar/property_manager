# Demo Flow (20 minutes)

## 1. Discovery (2 min)

**Ask:** "How do you manage leases and maintenance today?"

Listen for pain points: manual lease creation, WhatsApp chaos, tenant complaints, slow supplier response, owner reporting burden.

## 2. AI Lease Generation (5 min)

**Show:**
1. Open the Lease Builder
2. Select a property and template
3. Chat with Claude AI: "Generate a 12-month residential lease for a 2-bedroom flat in Melville, Johannesburg"
4. Watch the lease generate with merge fields auto-populated
5. Show the clause library and how clauses can be swapped
6. Quick edit in the TipTap editor
7. Export to PDF

**Key message:** "That took 3 minutes. How long does it take you today?"

## 3. Supplier Matching & Dispatch (5 min)

**Show:**
1. Create a maintenance request: "Leaking geyser in Unit 4B"
2. AI classifies it as Urgent / Plumbing
3. Show the 5-factor supplier ranking (proximity, skills, price, preference, rating)
4. Dispatch to top-ranked supplier
5. Show the real-time status updates across all 4 apps

**Key message:** "The tenant reported it, AI classified it, and the right supplier was notified — all without a phone call."

## 4. Tenant Mobile App (3 min)

**Show:**
1. Open the Flutter app (or simulator)
2. Report an issue with a photo
3. Show the real-time chat between tenant and agent
4. Show lease details and payment history in the app
5. Show the e-signing flow from the tenant's perspective

**Key message:** "Your tenants get a professional experience. They stop calling you at midnight."

## 5. Owner Portal (2 min)

**Show:**
1. Open the owner dashboard
2. Show occupancy rates, income tracking, maintenance pipeline
3. Show how it updates in real-time

**Key message:** "Your landlords see everything they need without you sending monthly reports."

## 6. Pricing & Close (3 min)

**Say:** "All of this is free for up to 5 units. No credit card. You can sign up right now and start using it today."

**If >5 units:** "The Professional tier is R49/unit — for 20 properties that's R980/month. Less than what you'd pay a part-time admin for one morning."

**Close:** "Want me to set up your account now, or would you prefer to explore the free tier on your own first?"
