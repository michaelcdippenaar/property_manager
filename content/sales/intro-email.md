## Usage notes

- Maximum two outbound touches per contact before marking as no-engage.
- Do not use scraped mobile numbers. Email only for cold outreach.
- Personalise [AREA] from the agency's publicly listed address — not from aggregated data sources.
- Log opt-outs in CRM within 24 hours of receipt.
- POPIA s69: direct marketing to juristic persons (companies) requires only an opt-out path, not prior consent. This email satisfies that. Direct marketing to natural persons (individuals) requires prior consent or an existing relationship — confirm this before sending to personal email addresses.
