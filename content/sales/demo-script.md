# Klikk Rentals — 15-Minute Demo Script

_For use by sales reps and founders. Scene-by-scene guide for a live product demo. All features shown are BUILT and available. Last updated: 2026-04-22._

---

## Before the call

- Confirm the prospect's current setup: How many units? What tools do they use today?
- Know their ICP tier (T1 boutique agency, T2 self-managing landlord, or T3 rental department agency — see `content/sales/icp.md`).
- Have the Klikk Admin dashboard open in a browser tab with a pre-seeded demo account (properties, leases, maintenance tickets, tenants loaded).
- Have the Klikk Agent mobile app open on a phone or simulator.
- Keep a timer — 15 minutes is strict. Discovery eats into it if you let it.

---

## Scene 1 — Discovery (2 minutes)

**Goal:** Diagnose their pain before showing anything. If you open the product first, you're doing a product tour, not a sales demo.

**Ask:**

> "Before I show you anything — how do you create leases today? Walk me through what happens from the moment you find a tenant to the moment they sign."

Listen for these signals:
- "Word template we got from a lawyer years ago" → RHA compliance gap
- "WhatsApp the PDF and hope they sign it" → no proper e-signing, no audit trail
- "It takes 2–3 days to get a lease back signed" → speed and friction
- "We email the tenant and then follow up again and again" → manual follow-up

**Then ask:**

> "And maintenance — how does a tenant report a problem, and how does it get resolved?"

Listen for:
- "WhatsApp me directly" → no triage, no trail
- "I have a guy I call" → no supplier matching, no escalation process
- "Owners complain they never know what's happening" → owner visibility gap

**Transition:**

> "Let me show you how Klikk handles all of that. I'll take you through a live account — this is a real property portfolio, not a mockup."

---

## Scene 2 — AI Lease Generation (4 minutes)

**Goal:** Show the single most compelling feature first. This is your hook.

**What to show:**

1. Open **Lease Builder** in the Klikk Admin dashboard.
2. Select an existing property from the demo account.
3. Pick a lease template (12-month residential — standard).
4. Open the AI chat prompt. Type:

   > "Generate a 12-month residential lease for a 2-bedroom flat in Melville, Johannesburg. Monthly rent R12,500. Deposit R25,000. Move-in date 1 June 2026."

5. Watch the lease generate with merge fields populated — tenant name, property address, rent amount, dates, deposit clause.
6. Click into the clause library: show 3–4 pre-approved clauses (pet policy, maintenance responsibility, utility obligations).
7. Make one quick edit in the TipTap editor — change a clause, add a sentence. Show how it works like Google Docs.
8. Click "Export to PDF" — show the PDF preview.

**Say at step 4:**

> "The AI drafts from your template. Every merge field pulls from the property and tenant records. You don't type the same details seven times across a Word doc anymore."

**Say at step 7:**

> "You approve every word. The AI is the first draft — you're the lawyer. Any clause is editable, and you can save your preferred clauses to the library so you never retype them."

**Key message:**

> "That took under 3 minutes. How long does it take you today?"

**If they ask about RHA compliance:**

> "The templates are built for the Rental Housing Act. Clauses covering deposit obligations, maintenance responsibilities, notice periods, and tenant rights are all there. You can customise, but you start from a compliant base."

---

## Scene 3 — Native E-Signing (2 minutes)

**Goal:** Show the seamless signing experience before jumping to maintenance.

**What to show:**

1. From the lease just generated, click **Send for Signing**.
2. Show the signer list — tenant, agent. Sequence is configurable.
3. Open the tenant signing link in a separate browser tab (simulate the tenant experience).
4. Show the signing interface — document display, signature capture field, the "sign here" prompt.
5. Show the real-time status update in the Admin dashboard as the signature lands.

**Say:**

> "The tenant gets a link on their phone. No app download, no DocuSign subscription required. They sign, you get a notification. The countersigned PDF is stored automatically."

**Say:**

> "This is also how rental mandates work — owner and agent both sign digitally before a property goes under management."

**Key message:**

> "No more printing, scanning, or chasing a PDF over WhatsApp."

---

## Scene 4 — Maintenance Triage and Supplier Dispatch (3 minutes)

**Goal:** Show the operational backbone — the feature that saves the most day-to-day time.

**What to show:**

1. Navigate to **Maintenance** in the Admin dashboard.
2. Show an existing ticket in the demo account (e.g. "Leaking geyser — Unit 4B, Cape Town property").
3. Show the AI classification: **Urgent / Plumbing**.
4. Open the supplier ranking panel. Show the five-factor score: proximity (2.1 km), trade match (plumber, verified), price history (avg R850 callout), owner preference (preferred supplier), rating (4.8 stars).
5. Click **Dispatch** — show the supplier notification going out.
6. Show the status timeline: Reported → Classified → Dispatched → In Progress.

**Say at step 3:**

> "The AI reads the description and classifies it by urgency and trade. You don't have to triage every ticket manually."

**Say at step 4:**

> "Suppliers are ranked on five factors. You can override it — but most of the time the top result is the right call."

**Key message:**

> "The tenant reported it in the app, the AI classified it, the right supplier was notified. No phone call from you. Full audit trail for the owner."

**If they ask about the supplier portal:**

> "Suppliers get their own portal — they receive job notifications, submit quotes, and manage their calendar. They don't need a Klikk login to quote. They click a link, fill in the form, done."

---

## Scene 5 — Tenant Mobile App (2 minutes)

**Goal:** Show the tenant experience. This differentiates Klikk from every SA competitor.

**What to show — on phone or simulator:**

1. Open the **Klikk Tenant app** on a phone (iOS or Android).
2. Navigate to **Report Issue** — show the photo attachment and description field.
3. Open **Chat** — show the AI tenant chat. Ask a property-specific question: "What is the notice period in my lease?" The AI answers from the lease terms on file.
4. Navigate to **Lease** — show the active lease summary, rent amount, move-in date.
5. Show the **Sign** prompt — a pending document awaiting tenant signature.

**Say at step 3:**

> "The chat is connected to your property's knowledge base — lease terms, house rules, appliance manuals if you upload them. Tenants get answers instantly without calling you. It's property-aware AI, not a generic chatbot."

**Key message:**

> "Your tenants get a professional experience. They stop calling you at midnight about things that are already in their lease."

---

## Scene 6 — Owner Dashboard (1 minute)

**Goal:** Show the owner visibility story. Quick — this is a supporting feature, not the hook.

**What to show:**

1. Open the **Owner Dashboard** (switch user context or show in a separate tab).
2. Show the portfolio summary: properties listed, occupancy rate, monthly income, pending maintenance.
3. Click into one property: lease timeline, rent tracking, maintenance pipeline.

**Say:**

> "This is what your landlords see. They can log in anytime and see their investment — no monthly report from you, no WhatsApp update requests. Read-only, real-time."

---

## Scene 7 — Pricing and Close (1 minute)

**Goal:** Remove the cost objection before they raise it. Be direct.

**Say:**

> "Pricing is simple. Free for up to five properties — no credit card, you can sign up right now and start today. The Pro tier is R2,500 per month for up to 50 properties or 100 units. That's unlimited AI leases, all four apps, e-signing, maintenance — everything we just looked at."

**Pause. Then:**

> "For a 20-unit portfolio, that is R125 per unit per month. Less than what you're already spending on software that doesn't do any of this."

**Close options — pick one based on their energy:**

| If they seem ready | Say |
|--------------------|-----|
| Highly engaged, technical | "Want to set up your account now? I can walk you through importing your first property." |
| Interested but cautious | "Start on the free tier. Five properties, full AI suite. See if it fits your workflow before committing to anything." |
| Needs time | "I'll send you the one-pager and a direct signup link. If you want a follow-up in a week, just say the word." |

## Objection handling during the demo

> "We already use PayProp."

"Great — Klikk complements PayProp. PayProp handles payments; Klikk handles everything before and after the payment — leases, maintenance, tenant comms, owner reporting. We're building a direct integration."

> "Is the AI actually reliable for leases?"

"The AI drafts, you approve. Klikk uses Claude AI — the same model running inside many SA legal and compliance tools. But the answer isn't 'trust the AI' — it's that you review every word and every clause before it goes out. The AI saves you the drafting time."

> "What if we have more than 5 properties?"

"The free tier covers five properties to get you started. Pro is R2,500/month for up to 50 properties or 100 units — unlimited everything."
