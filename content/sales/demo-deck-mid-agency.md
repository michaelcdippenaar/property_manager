## Slide 11 — Objection Handlers

### "We already use PayProp."
PayProp handles payments brilliantly. Klikk handles the workflow PayProp doesn't touch: leases, mandate signing, maintenance dispatch, tenant communication, owner portal, and arrears tracking. They work together. We're building a direct integration.

### "What happens to our data if we leave?"
Your data is yours. Export leases and tenant records at any time in standard formats. We don't hold your data hostage. POPIA requires us to provide access and portability.

### "How do we migrate from WeConnectU / Red Rabbit?"
We import your existing lease and tenant data. The migration takes one day with our help — not a project. Early adopters get free assisted migration.

### "Is AI-generated content legal for a lease?"
The AI generates a draft. You — a registered professional — review, edit, and approve it before it goes out. The output is your document. ECTA s13 recognises electronic signatures. RHA compliance is built into the templates.

### "POPIA — where is our data?"
All data is hosted in South Africa on SA-jurisdiction infrastructure. Encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3). Role-based access: tenants see only their data, owners see only their properties. Full audit trail. No data shared with third parties.

### "We've never heard of Klikk."
We're new. Built by a landlord who needed it. That means you get direct access to the team, your feedback shapes the roadmap, and you're not paying for a decade of legacy code. The free trial costs nothing to test.
