# Objection Handling

## "We use PayProp"

**Response:** Great — PayProp handles payments brilliantly. Klikk manages everything PayProp doesn't: AI lease generation, maintenance dispatch, tenant communication, and e-signing. We're building a direct integration so they work together seamlessly. Think of Klikk as the management layer on top of PayProp's payment engine.

## "Don't trust AI for leases"

**Response:** Completely understandable. The AI drafts the lease — you review and edit every word before it goes out. Think of it as a fast paralegal, not a replacement for your judgement. Every clause is editable in our rich text editor, and you can build a library of approved clauses to reuse. The lease templates are RHA-compliant out of the box.

## "Too expensive"

**Response:** At R49/unit, managing 10 properties costs R490/month — less than one hour of admin time. And the first 5 units are free forever. Most clients save 5+ admin hours per month. We've seen property managers reclaim entire mornings that used to be spent chasing maintenance and creating leases manually.

## "Never heard of you"

**Response:** We're new and deliberately SA-focused. Built by a landlord who couldn't find a tool that understood South African rental law. Early adopters get the most input into the roadmap and the most support. Our free tier means zero risk to try.

## "WeConnectU does everything we need"

**Response:** WeConnectU is a solid platform for large agencies. But for portfolios under 100 units, you're paying enterprise prices for features you don't use. Klikk offers similar breadth — AI lease generation, maintenance, tenant app, owner portal — at a fraction of the cost, with a mobile-first tenant experience WeConnectU doesn't have.

## "We're happy with Excel and WhatsApp"

**Response:** That works until it doesn't — a lost WhatsApp message, a lease dispute over a missing clause, a maintenance job that falls through the cracks. Klikk doesn't replace your workflow, it organises it. Free for up to 5 units, so you can try it alongside what you're doing now.

## "We need to see it first"

**Response:** Absolutely. I can do a 15-minute walkthrough right now, or you can sign up for free and explore on your own. No credit card, no demo call required. Start at klikk.co.za.

## "What about data security?"

**Response:** All data is hosted in South Africa, encrypted at rest and in transit. We're POPIA compliant. JWT authentication, role-based access control — tenants can never see other tenants' data, and owners only see their own properties.
