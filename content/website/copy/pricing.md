## FAQ

**Q: Is there really a free plan?**
A: Yes. Manage up to 5 units free forever. Full platform access, AI lease generation (5/month), and the tenant mobile app. No credit card required.

**Q: What happens when I exceed 5 units?**
A: You'll be prompted to upgrade to Professional. Your existing data stays intact — just pick a plan and continue.

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there an annual discount?**
A: Yes — 15% off when you pay annually.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit card, debit card, and EFT for annual plans.

**Q: Do you offer a trial of Professional/Agency?**
A: We offer a free tier instead of a trial. Start with 5 units, experience the full platform, and upgrade when you're ready.

**Q: What's included in "unlimited AI"?**
A: Unlimited AI lease generation, clause generation, document parsing, and maintenance triage. No per-use fees.
