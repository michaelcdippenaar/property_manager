# About Page Copy

## Headline
Built by a landlord who needed it.

## Story
I manage my own residential properties in South Africa. Like most landlords, I started with spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper leases. Then came the lease disputes, the midnight maintenance calls, and the realisation that no existing tool understood South African rental law.

So I built Klikk.

Not another generic property management platform adapted for SA. A purpose-built system that understands the Rental Housing Act, generates compliant leases with AI, matches the right supplier to every maintenance job, and gives every person in the rental lifecycle — tenant, agent, owner, supplier — their own tailored experience.

## Mission
Make professional property management accessible to every South African landlord and agent — whether you manage 1 unit or 100.

## Values

**SA-first:** Every feature is built for South African rental law, compliance, and market practices.

**AI-native:** Intelligence is woven into every workflow, not bolted on as an afterthought.

**Honest:** We show what's built and what's coming. No vaporware, no overpromising.

**Accessible:** A free tier that's genuinely useful, not a teaser. Professional tools shouldn't require enterprise budgets.
