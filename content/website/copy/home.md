#### Agent Admin Dashboard
**Tagline:** Your whole portfolio, at a glance.
**Body:** Manage leases, maintenance tickets, tenant onboarding, and owner reporting from a single dashboard. Everything is real time. Nothing falls through the cracks.

#### Klikk Agent App (iOS + Android)
**Tagline:** Manage on the go.
**Body:** Browse your assigned properties, book and manage viewings, capture prospect details, and track your pipeline — from your phone, anywhere.

#### Tenant App (iOS + Android)
**Tagline:** Their app. Your fewer phone calls.
**Body:** Tenants report issues with photos, chat with an AI that knows their property, sign documents, and view their lease — all in one place. No more WhatsApp maintenance requests.

#### Owner Dashboard
**Tagline:** Their investment, visible 24/7.
**Body:** Property owners see occupancy, income tracking, maintenance status, and lease pipeline — read-only, real-time, and no login friction. Owners stop calling you for updates.

#### Supplier Portal
**Tagline:** Jobs in. Quotes out. No login required.
**Body:** Suppliers receive job notifications, submit quotes, and manage their calendar by clicking a link. No app download, no account creation, no friction in the maintenance loop.
