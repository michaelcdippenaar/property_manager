What happens if my landlord doesn't return my deposit in time?
According to your lease (§12.3) and the Rental Housing Act §5(3)(g), your landlord must refund your deposit within 14 days of lease termination (or 21 days if an inspection is needed). If they don't, you can lodge a complaint with the Rental Housing Tribunal.
📖 RHA §5(3)(g) — Deposit refund ⚖️ Tribunal Case 2023/287
What happens if my lease is not renewed?
Your lease (§8.1) converts to a month-to-month periodic tenancy on the same terms when it expires without renewal. Neither party can change conditions without written notice. Under the RHA, your landlord needs to give you 1 calendar month's written notice to terminate a periodic lease.
📖 Your Lease §8.1 — Renewal terms 📖 RHA §5(5) — Periodic tenancy
