# Title: Welcome to Klikk — Your Home, Sorted (60 seconds)
# Target viewer: New tenant, first app open after receiving invite from agent
# When shown: Immediately after first successful login, before Dashboard loads
# Caption file: tenant-welcome.vtt (to be produced alongside video)
# Voice-over artist direction: Friendly, reassuring, plain-spoken. SA-English.
  Think neighbour who actually knows their stuff. Not corporate, not chirpy.

---

## Production Notes

- Total runtime: 58–62 seconds
- Resolution: 1080 × 1920 (portrait), safe zone 1080 × 1700
- Colour palette: Navy #2B2D6E (backgrounds), Accent #FF3D7F (highlights), white copy
- Font: DM Sans — all on-screen text
- Screen recordings: use a demo tenant account. Show a completed lease, a maintenance ticket,
  and the AI chat. No placeholder screens, no "Lorem ipsum."
- Captions: every voice-over line has a matching caption bar at bottom of frame.
  Caption text matches voice-over word-for-word. No audio-only information.
- Music: same light background track as agent video, slightly warmer mix. Fades to silence
  by Scene 5.
- Tone note: tenants are often anxious about a new rental. The video should feel calm and
  reassuring — you've got this sorted for them.

## Scene 2 — Your Lease (0:08–0:22)

**Visual:**
Screen recording of the tenant app Lease section. The lease summary card is visible:
property address, tenant name, monthly rent amount (e.g. R 8 500), lease start and end dates.
Tenant taps the card. The lease PDF preview slides up — first page visible with their
name and address. The Signed badge (green tick) is shown clearly.

Zoom in on the Signed badge for a beat, then pull back to show the full summary card.

**Voice-over:**
"Your lease is saved here. You can read it any time — the rent amount, your move-in date,
and the end date of your fixed-term agreement. If you ever have a question about what
you signed, the answer is in this app."

Your lease — always accessible.
Rent · Dates · Your signed copy.

**Caption:**

## Scene 3 — Log a Maintenance Request (0:22–0:36)

**Visual:**
Screen recording. Tenant navigates to Maintenance tab. Empty-state is shown for a moment
("No open jobs — great!"). Tenant taps "Log a job." Simple form: select room (bedroom),
type a short description ("Tap dripping in bathroom"). Tenant attaches one photo
(camera roll picker opens, photo selected). Taps Submit. Confirmation screen: "Job logged.
Your agent has been notified." Status shows "Open."

Highlight each tap with a pink ripple.

**Voice-over:**
"If something needs fixing, log it here. Choose the room, describe the problem, add a photo.
Your agent gets notified immediately, and you can track the repair from start to finish — right
in the app."

**On-screen text:**
Something broken? Log it here.
Photo + description. We handle the rest.

**Caption:**
[If something needs fixing, log it here. Choose the room, describe the problem, add a photo.
Your agent gets notified immediately, and you can track the repair from start to finish — right
in the app.]

## Scene 4 — AI Chat (0:36–0:48)

**Visual:**
Screen recording. Tenant opens the Chat tab. The AI chat interface is open — a welcoming
first message from "Klikk AI" is visible: "Hi [Name], I know your property inside and out.
Ask me anything." Tenant types "When is my lease up?" The AI responds immediately with
the correct date pulled from their lease. Tenant types "What are the house rules about
pets?" AI responds with the relevant clause.

Camera sits on the chat for 5 seconds to let both exchanges land.

**Voice-over:**
"You also have a 24/7 AI assistant — it knows your property's lease, house rules, and
maintenance history. Ask it anything about your rental, any time."

AI assistant. Always on.
Ask anything about your home.
