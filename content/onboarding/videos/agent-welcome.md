# Title: Welcome to Klikk — Your First Rental Cycle Starts Here (90 seconds)
# Target viewer: New rental agent, first app open after account activation
# When shown: Immediately after first successful login, before Dashboard loads
# Caption file: agent-welcome.vtt (to be produced alongside video)
# Voice-over artist direction: Warm, confident, unhurried. Conversational SA-English.
  No "let's get started" energy. Think colleague showing you the ropes on day one.

---

## Production Notes

- Total runtime: 88–92 seconds
- Resolution: 1080 × 1920 (portrait), safe zone 1080 × 1700 (clear of notch and home indicator)
- Colour palette: Navy #2B2D6E (backgrounds), Accent #FF3D7F (highlights), white copy
- Font: DM Sans — all on-screen text
- Screen recordings: use the real agent app (Klikk Agent, iOS/Android). Record at 3x speed,
  slow to 1x on key taps. No placeholder screens.
- Captions: every voice-over line has a matching caption bar at the bottom of frame.
  Caption text matches voice-over word-for-word. No audio-only information anywhere.
- Music: light, low-key background track. Ducked under voice-over. Fades to silence by Scene 6.
