# How to Update Owner Dashboard Permissions

**Summary:** Control what your property owners can see on their dashboard — you decide which properties and data sections each owner can access.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## How owner access works on Klikk

Each property owner has their own read-only Owner Dashboard. They can only see the properties you have explicitly linked to their account. Owners cannot see financial details of other owners' properties and cannot make changes to any data — they can only view.

You manage their access from the Klikk Admin portal.

---

## Steps

### 1. Go to the owner's profile

Click **Owners** in the left navigation.

Find the owner by name or email. Click their name to open their profile.

> **Screenshot:** Owners list — table with name, email, number of properties, and a "View Profile" link.

---

### 2. Check which properties they can access

The **Properties** section of the owner profile shows all properties linked to this owner.

- A green tick means the property is visible to them on their dashboard.
- A grey dash means the property exists in the system but is not currently shared with this owner.

> **Screenshot:** Owner profile — Properties section with two properties ticked and one row with a grey dash.

---

### 3. Add a property to the owner's view

Click **"Add Property"** in the Properties section. A search box appears.

Type the property address and select it from the dropdown. Click **"Confirm"**.

The owner can now see that property on their dashboard the next time they log in.

> **Screenshot:** Add Property dropdown — search results showing two matching addresses.

---

### 4. Remove a property from the owner's view

Click the **"..."** icon next to the property you want to hide. Select **"Remove from Dashboard"**.

Confirm the action. The property is immediately hidden from the owner's view. The property record itself is not deleted — this only affects what the owner can see.

> **Screenshot:** Property row context menu — "Remove from Dashboard" option highlighted in the dropdown.

---

### 5. Control which data sections are visible

Within each property, you can toggle which sections the owner can see. On the property row, click **"Permissions"**.

| Section | Default | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Occupancy status | On | Who is in the property |
| Rent status | On | Whether rent is paid or in arrears |
| Maintenance issues | On | Open and resolved issues |
| Lease documents | Off | Full lease PDF (turn on with caution) |
| Financial reports | Off | Requires your explicit sign-off to enable |

Toggle each section on or off and click **"Save Permissions"**.

> **Screenshot:** Permissions panel for a property — toggle switches for each section, Lease documents and Financial reports currently off.

---

### 6. Resend the owner's invite

If an owner has not yet activated their account, you can resend the invite from their profile page:

1. Open the owner's profile.
2. Click **"Resend Invite"** at the top of the page.
3. The owner receives a fresh activation email.

> **Screenshot:** Owner profile — amber "Invite Pending" badge at the top, "Resend Invite" button next to it.

---

## Changing an owner's email address

If the owner's email address has changed:

1. Open their profile.
2. Click **"Edit"** at the top.
3. Update the email address field.
4. Click **"Save"**.
5. The owner will need to verify their new address before they can log in again.
