# How to Create a Mandate

**Summary:** A mandate is your legal agreement with the property owner — you must create and get it signed before you can manage any property on Klikk.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Before you start

- The property must already be added to your account.
- You need the owner's full name, email address, and ID or company registration number.
- You need to know which mandate type applies: Full Management, Letting Only, Rent Collection, or Finders Fee.

---

## Steps

### 1. Open the property

Go to **Properties** in the left navigation of the Klikk Admin portal (or tap **Properties** in the Klikk Agent app).

Find the property and click or tap it to open the property detail page.

> **Screenshot:** Property detail page — the "Mandate" tab is visible in the tab bar at the top of the page.

---

### 2. Go to the Mandate tab

Click the **Mandate** tab. If no mandate exists yet, you will see an empty state with a **"Create Mandate"** button.

> **Screenshot:** Mandate tab — empty state with the pink "Create Mandate" button.

---

### 3. Choose a mandate type

Click **"Create Mandate"**. A form slides in. Select the mandate type from the dropdown:

- **Full Management** — You handle everything: finding tenants, leases, rent, maintenance, renewals.
- **Letting Only** — You find the tenant and sign the lease. The owner takes over after move-in.
- **Rent Collection** — Tenant is already in place. You collect and disburse rent only.
- **Finders Fee** — One-off fee for finding a tenant. No ongoing management.

> **Screenshot:** Mandate type dropdown — "Full Management" selected.

---

### 4. Fill in the mandate details

Complete the remaining fields:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Owner full name | As it appears on their ID or registration document |
| Owner email | The address they will use to sign |
| Commission % | Your agreed percentage (default pre-fills from agency settings) |
| Monthly fee (if applicable) | For Rent Collection or Full Management types |
| Start date | When the mandate takes effect |

---

### 5. Save and send for signing

Click **"Save and Send"**. Klikk generates the mandate PDF and emails a signing link to the owner.

You will also receive a copy of the email for your records.

> **Screenshot:** Success toast — "Mandate sent to [Owner name] for signing."

---

### 6. Wait for owner signature

The owner clicks the link in their email and signs on any device — no account needed. You will get an in-app notification and email confirmation once they have signed.

> **Screenshot:** Mandate tab — status badge showing "Awaiting owner signature."

---

### 7. Counter-sign as agent

Once the owner has signed, the mandate returns to you for counter-signature. Open the Mandate tab again and click **"Sign Mandate"** to add your signature.

After both parties have signed, the PDF is locked and stored under the Mandate tab. The property status updates to **Active**.

> **Screenshot:** Mandate tab — countersigned mandate with download button and "Signed by both parties" status badge.

---

## What happens next

With an active mandate in place you can:

- Add the owner to the Owner portal
- Book property viewings
- Generate a lease for an approved tenant
