# How to Invite a Tenant

**Summary:** Once you have a signed lease ready, send the tenant an invite link so they can access their Klikk Tenant account and sign their lease.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Before you start

- The lease must exist and be in **Draft** or **Pending Signing** status. See [How to generate a lease](how-to-generate-a-lease.md).
- You need the tenant's full name and a valid email address or mobile number.
- The tenant must be 18 or older and have a valid SA ID or passport number.

---

## Steps

### 1. Open the lease

Go to **Leases** in the Klikk Admin portal and find the lease for the property. Click it to open the lease detail page.

> **Screenshot:** Lease detail page — the "Tenants" section is visible below the lease header.

---

### 2. Add the tenant

In the **Tenants** section, click **"Add Tenant"**. Enter:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Full name | Tenant's legal name |
| Email address | Where the invite will be sent |
| Mobile number | Optional — used for SMS backup |
| ID / Passport number | Required for the lease |
| Date of birth | Required for the lease |

Click **"Save Tenant"**.

> **Screenshot:** Add Tenant form with fields filled in.

---

### 3. Send the invite

After saving the tenant, click **"Send Invite"** next to their name. Klikk sends an email (and SMS if a mobile number was provided) with a secure link.

The link:
- Lets the tenant create their Klikk account (or log in if they already have one).
- Connects them to this specific property and lease.
- Is valid for 7 days. You can resend it at any time.

> **Screenshot:** Tenants section — "Invite sent" badge next to the tenant name, with a "Resend Invite" button.

---

### 4. Confirm the tenant has accepted

Once the tenant clicks the link and activates their account, the badge changes to **"Active"**. You will also get an in-app notification.

> **Screenshot:** Tenants section — "Active" badge next to the tenant name.

---

### 5. Proceed to signing

With the tenant active, you can now send the lease for digital signing. See [How to send a lease for signing](how-to-send-for-signing.md).

---

## Resending an invite

If the tenant did not receive the email:

1. Ask them to check their spam or junk folder first.
2. If still not found, click **"Resend Invite"** from the Tenants section.
3. If the email address was wrong, click the pencil icon next to the tenant name to correct it, then resend.
