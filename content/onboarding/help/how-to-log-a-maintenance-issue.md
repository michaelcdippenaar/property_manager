# How to Log a Maintenance Issue

**Summary:** Log a maintenance request from the admin portal or the Agent app, and Klikk will classify the issue, suggest the right supplier, and track it through to resolution.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Who can log a maintenance issue?

| Role | Where |
|---|---|
| **Agent** | Klikk Admin portal (browser) or Klikk Agent mobile app |
| **Tenant** | Klikk Tenant app — issues go directly into your queue |
| **Owner** | Owner portal — owners can view issues but agents log them |

---

## Steps (Admin portal)

### 1. Go to Maintenance

Click **Maintenance** in the left navigation.

> **Screenshot:** Maintenance list page — table of open issues with status badges (Open, In Progress, Resolved).

---

### 2. Click "New Issue"

Click the pink **"New Issue"** button in the top right corner.

> **Screenshot:** Maintenance page — "New Issue" button highlighted.

---

### 3. Select the property and unit

Start typing the property address in the search field. Select the correct property from the dropdown. If the property has multiple units, select the unit.

> **Screenshot:** Property search field with dropdown results showing two matching properties.

---

### 4. Describe the issue

Fill in the issue details:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Short description, e.g. "Burst pipe under kitchen sink" |
| **Description** | More detail — what is broken, when it started, any temporary measures taken |
| **Category** | Plumbing, Electrical, General, Structural, Garden, Appliance, etc. |
| **Priority** | Emergency, High, Medium, Low |
| **Photos** | Attach up to 5 photos (tap the camera icon or drag and drop) |

> **Screenshot:** New issue form — Title, Description, Category, and Priority fields filled in.

---

### 5. Let AI classify (optional)

Click **"AI Assist"** and Klikk's AI will read your description and suggest a category and priority. You can accept or override the suggestion.

> **Screenshot:** AI Assist result — "Category: Plumbing | Priority: High" with Accept and Override buttons.

---

### 6. Save the issue

Click **"Save Issue"**. The issue is created with status **"Open"** and appears in the maintenance queue.

Klikk notifies the tenant (if one is linked to the unit) that the issue has been logged.

> **Screenshot:** Issue detail page — status badge "Open", supplier section showing "No supplier assigned yet."

---

### 7. Assign a supplier (optional at this step)

You can assign a supplier immediately or wait for Klikk's supplier matching to suggest one. See the supplier matching panel on the right side of the issue detail page.

To assign manually, click **"Assign Supplier"** and search by trade, name, or rating.

---

## Steps (Klikk Agent mobile app)

1. Tap the **Maintenance** tab at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap the **+** button.
3. Select the property and unit, fill in title and description, add photos with the camera.
4. Tap **"Save"**.

> **Screenshot:** Agent app — New maintenance issue screen on a mobile device.

---

## Tracking an issue after it is logged

Open any issue to see:

- **Status history** — a timeline from Open through to Resolved.
- **Supplier updates** — quotes, job acceptance, work completed notes.
- **Tenant messages** — any chat messages from the tenant about this issue.
- **Photos** — before and after photos attached by the supplier.
