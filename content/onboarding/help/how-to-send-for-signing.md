# How to Send a Lease for Signing

**Summary:** Once the lease is drafted and the tenant is invited, send the document to all parties for digital signing — no printing, no couriers, no chasing.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Before you start

- The lease must be in **Draft** status with at least one tenant added.
- All tenant email addresses must be confirmed (the invite must have been sent and accepted, or the tenant is already an active Klikk user).
- Review the lease one more time before sending — you cannot edit it once it is out for signing.

---

## Steps

### 1. Open the lease

Go to **Leases** and click the lease you want to send. Confirm the status badge reads **"Draft"**.

> **Screenshot:** Lease list — a lease card with a "Draft" status badge in grey.

---

### 2. Click "Send for Signing"

On the lease detail page, click the **"Send for Signing"** button. A confirmation dialog shows the signing order and the email addresses that will receive links.

**Default signing order:**
1. Tenant(s) — each tenant signs independently.
2. Agent — you sign after all tenants have signed.

> **Screenshot:** Confirmation dialog — signing order list with two entries: "Tenant — [email]" and "Agent — [your email]".

---

### 3. Confirm and send

Click **"Confirm and Send"**. Klikk:

- Locks the lease document so it cannot be changed.
- Sends a signing link to the first party in the queue (the tenant).
- Changes the lease status to **"Pending Signing"**.

You will receive an email confirming the lease has been sent.

> **Screenshot:** Lease detail page — status badge now reads "Pending Signing" in amber.

---

### 4. Track signing progress

The **Signing Progress** panel on the lease detail page shows who has signed and who is still outstanding.

| Icon | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Grey circle | Has not opened the signing link yet |
| Amber circle | Has opened the link but not yet signed |
| Green tick | Signed — timestamp recorded |

> **Screenshot:** Signing Progress panel — tenant showing an amber circle (opened, not signed), agent showing a grey circle (not yet sent their link).

---

### 5. Agent counter-signature

Once all tenants have signed, Klikk automatically sends you a signing link. Click it (or go to the lease and click **"Sign Now"**) to add your signature.

After your signature, the lease is complete. Klikk:

- Locks the PDF with a tamper-evident seal.
- Emails the fully signed PDF to all parties.
- Updates the lease status to **"Active"**.
- Updates the unit status to **"Occupied"**.

> **Screenshot:** Lease detail page — status badge reads "Active" in green, signed PDF download button visible.

---

## Signing on the tenant's side

The tenant receives an email with a link. They do not need to create an account or install anything. The signing page works on any phone, tablet, or computer.

- They read through the lease on-screen.
- They draw or type their signature.
- They tick the confirmation checkbox and click **"Sign"**.
