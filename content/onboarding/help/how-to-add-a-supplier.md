# How to Add a Supplier

**Summary:** Add tradespeople and service providers to your supplier list so Klikk can match and dispatch them when maintenance issues come in.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Who are suppliers on Klikk?

Suppliers are the tradespeople, contractors, and service providers you use to fix things at your properties — plumbers, electricians, general maintenance teams, garden services, locksmiths, and so on.

Once a supplier is in your account, Klikk's matching algorithm can assign them to maintenance jobs based on their trade, location, and rating.

---

## Steps

### 1. Go to Suppliers

Click **Suppliers** in the left navigation of the Klikk Admin portal.

> **Screenshot:** Suppliers list page — a table with columns for name, trade, area, and rating.

---

### 2. Click "Add Supplier"

Click the pink **"Add Supplier"** button in the top right corner.

> **Screenshot:** Suppliers page — "Add Supplier" button in the top right, pink.

---

### 3. Fill in the supplier details

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Business name** | Trading name (e.g. "Fast Fix Plumbing") |
| **Contact person** | Name of the person who handles job requests |
| **Email address** | For job notifications and quote requests |
| **Mobile number** | For SMS notifications |
| **Trade(s)** | Select one or more (Plumbing, Electrical, General, etc.) |
| **Service area** | The suburb(s) or city areas they cover |
| **VAT registered** | Tick if applicable — affects invoice handling |
| **Rate type** | Hourly, call-out fee, or quote-based |

> **Screenshot:** Add Supplier form — all fields visible, Trade dropdown open showing Plumbing, Electrical, General, Structural, Garden, Appliance, Locksmith.

---

### 4. Save the supplier

Click **"Save Supplier"**. The supplier is added to your list with an **"Active"** status.

> **Screenshot:** Supplier detail page — name, trade badges, area, rating (shown as "No ratings yet"), and an "Invite to Portal" button.

---

### 5. Invite the supplier to the Supplier Portal (optional but recommended)

Klikk has a Supplier Portal where suppliers can receive job notifications, submit quotes, update job status, and attach completion photos — without you having to chase them by phone.

To invite them:

1. Click **"Invite to Portal"** on the supplier detail page.
2. Enter their email address (pre-filled if already saved).
3. Click **"Send Invite"**.

The supplier gets an email with a login link. They do not pay anything — it is free for suppliers.

> **Screenshot:** Invite to Portal dialog — email field with the supplier's address pre-filled, "Send Invite" button.

---

### 6. Supplier is now available for job matching

When you log a new maintenance issue, Klikk's matching engine will include this supplier in its suggestions when the issue matches their trade and service area.

You can also assign them manually from any issue's **Supplier** panel.

---

## Editing or deactivating a supplier

To edit a supplier's details:
1. Go to **Suppliers**, find the supplier, and click their name.
2. Click **"Edit"** and update the relevant fields.

To deactivate a supplier (e.g. they are no longer available):
1. Open their detail page.
2. Click the **"..."** menu → **"Deactivate"**.
3. They will no longer appear in matching results but their history is preserved.
