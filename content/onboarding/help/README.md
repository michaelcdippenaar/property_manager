# Klikk Agent Help Centre

Step-by-step guides for rental agents in their first 30 days on Klikk.

Each article covers one task from start to finish — no jargon, no assumed knowledge.

---

## Articles

| # | Article | What it covers |
|---|---------|----------------|
| 1 | [How to create a mandate](how-to-create-a-mandate.md) | Set up the owner agreement before managing any property |
| 2 | [How to invite a tenant](how-to-invite-a-tenant.md) | Send a tenant their Klikk account invite and link them to a lease |
| 3 | [How to generate a lease](how-to-generate-a-lease.md) | Use the AI lease builder to produce a compliant residential lease |
| 4 | [How to send a lease for signing](how-to-send-for-signing.md) | Get the lease to all parties for digital signature |

| 6 | [How to log a maintenance issue](how-to-log-a-maintenance-issue.md) | Record a repair request from the portal or the Agent app |
| 7 | [How to see rent status](how-to-see-rent-status.md) | Check who has paid, mark payments, and download receipts |
| 8 | [How to renew a lease](how-to-renew-a-lease.md) | Agree new terms and get a renewal signed before expiry |
| 9 | [How to add a supplier](how-to-add-a-supplier.md) | Add a tradesperson and invite them to the Supplier Portal |
