# How to See Rent Status

**Summary:** Klikk tracks rent payments against each active lease so you can see at a glance who has paid, who is in arrears, and generate receipts for any payment.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Where to find rent status

There are three ways to check rent status:

| Method | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard overview** | Quick snapshot across all properties |
| **Lease detail page** | Full payment history for one tenant |
| **Rent report** | Exporting a list for the owner or for trust accounting |

---

## Option 1 — Dashboard overview

### 1. Open the Dashboard

Click **Dashboard** in the left navigation (or the home icon in the Agent app).

The **Rent Summary** tile shows:
- Number of leases where rent is confirmed this month
- Number in arrears
- Number pending (payment reference issued but no confirmation yet)

> **Screenshot:** Dashboard — Rent Summary tile with three numbers: Paid (green), Pending (amber), Arrears (red).

---

### 2. Click a status to drill down

Click **"Arrears"** to see a filtered list of tenants with outstanding rent. Click any row to go directly to that lease's payment history.

---

## Option 2 — Lease detail page

### 1. Open the lease

Go to **Leases** and click the active lease for the tenant you want to check.

---

### 2. Click the "Payments" tab

The **Payments** tab shows a table of all rent periods for this lease:

| Column | What it means |
|---|---|
| Period | Month and year (e.g. May 2026) |
| Due date | When rent was due |
| Amount | Monthly rental amount |
| Status | Paid / Pending / Overdue |
| Reference | Unique payment reference for this tenant |
| Receipt | Download link (available once payment is confirmed) |

> **Screenshot:** Payments tab — three rows, two marked "Paid" with a green tick and one marked "Overdue" in red.

---

### 3. Mark a payment as received

If a tenant pays by EFT into your trust account, Klikk does not receive the bank notification automatically — you mark it manually:

1. Click the row for the relevant month.
2. Click **"Mark as Paid"**.
3. Enter the payment date and any reference from your bank statement.
4. Click **"Confirm"**.

The status updates to **Paid** and a receipt is generated automatically.

> **Screenshot:** Mark as Paid dialog — payment date field and reference field.

---

### 4. Download a receipt

Once a payment is marked as paid, a **"Download Receipt"** link appears in the row. Click it to download a PDF receipt you can forward to the tenant.

---

## Option 3 — Rent report

1. Go to **Reports** in the left navigation.
2. Select **"Rent Status Report"**.
3. Choose the date range (e.g. current month, last 3 months, custom).
4. Click **"Generate"**. The report downloads as a PDF or CSV.

> **Screenshot:** Reports page — "Rent Status Report" option selected, date range picker visible.

---

## Payment references

Every tenant gets a unique payment reference when their lease goes active (e.g. `KLK-0042-MAY26`). This reference never changes. When tenants pay by EFT, they should use this reference so you can match payments quickly.

To find a tenant's payment reference: go to the Payments tab on their lease — the reference is in the column header and on every payment row.
