## What Klikk Does (Built Features Only)

### 1. AI Lease Generation
Draft a Rental Housing Act-compliant lease in under 5 minutes. Chat with Claude AI to generate the document, auto-populate all tenant and property fields, then edit every clause in the built-in rich text editor. Export to PDF. No Word templates. No manual merge fields. No cut-and-paste errors.

> "That took 3 minutes. How long does it take your team today?"

### 2. Digital Signing — No Third-Party Subscription
Tenants and owners sign from any phone or computer via a secure link. No DocuSign account. No extra licence fee. Track completion in real time. Countersigned PDFs stored automatically on the property record. This covers both lease signing and rental mandate signing between your agency and the property owner.

### 3. Rental Mandate E-Signing
Generate and sign the rental mandate (Full Management, Letting Only, or Rent Collection) before a property goes live. Owner signs on their phone. You sign in the admin portal. Both parties get a countersigned copy. Required by law — handled in seconds.

### 4. Rent Tracking and Receipts
Every tenant gets a unique payment reference. Mark payments received, generate receipts, and flag arrears automatically. No more cross-referencing bank statements with a spreadsheet. Owners see income in their portal the moment you mark a payment.

### 5. Owner Dashboard
Your landlords log in and see occupancy, income, maintenance status, and lease pipeline — updated in real time. Stop spending Friday afternoons writing owner reports that take longer to compile than to read.

## Compliance Built In

- **POPIA:** All tenant and owner data hosted in South Africa. Role-based access control — tenants see only their own data. Full audit trail on every action.
- **RHA:** Lease templates built to Rental Housing Act requirements. Payment tracking, deposit and arrears records, lease renewal reminders.
- **ECTA s13:** Native e-signatures are legally valid under the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act.
