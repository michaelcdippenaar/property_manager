# Competitive Landscape

## Direct Competitors

### PayProp
- **What they do:** Rent collection, trust accounting, payment processing
- **Strength:** Dominant in SA payment processing, trusted by agents
- **Weakness:** Payments only — no lease management, no maintenance, no tenant app
- **Our play:** "The AI brain on top of PayProp's payment engine." Klikk manages everything before and after the payment. Integration planned (REST API v1.1, OAuth 2.0).
- **URL:** payprop.co.za

### WeConnectU
- **What they do:** Full rental asset management platform
- **Strength:** Comprehensive, established in enterprise market
- **Weakness:** Expensive, complex, slow to adopt AI, no mobile tenant app
- **Our play:** Similar breadth but AI-native, mobile-first, and affordable for small portfolios. Their clients with 10-50 units are overpaying.
- **URL:** weconnectu.co.za

### Prop Data (MRI Software)
- **What they do:** Property CRM and management
- **Strength:** Enterprise features, MRI backing
- **Weakness:** Legacy tech, expensive, no AI, no SA-specific compliance focus
- **Our play:** Modern alternative for SA-focused managers who don't need enterprise complexity.

### TPN Credit Bureau
- **What they do:** Tenant screening (credit, criminal, ID, rental history)
- **Strength:** SA's go-to screening provider
- **Weakness:** Screening only — manual integration with everything else
- **Our play:** Integrate TPN directly into Klikk's onboarding flow. "Screen tenants in 60 seconds, without leaving Klikk."

## Failing/Weak Competitors

### RentMaster
- **Status:** Declining, outdated interface
- **Opportunity:** Their users are looking for modern alternatives

### HouseME
- **Status:** Struggling, limited feature set
- **Opportunity:** Capture their dissatisfied users with a free tier

## Indirect Competition

### Spreadsheets + WhatsApp + Email
- **Reality:** This is how most small SA landlords manage today
- **Our play:** "You're already doing the work. Klikk just organises it."

### Property agencies (manual management)
- **Reality:** Many agencies still use paper files and verbal agreements
- **Our play:** Free tier removes the cost barrier to digitisation
