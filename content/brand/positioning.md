# Klikk Rentals — Brand Positioning

_Pricing references in this document use flat-rate tiers from `content/product/pricing.yaml`. No per-unit pricing applies to Klikk — we charge per organisation, not per unit._

---

## Positioning statement

**Klikk is the only AI-native rental management platform built for South African law** — serving boutique rental agencies (2–10 agents) and self-managing landlords who want to run a full rental portfolio from a single platform without a developer on staff.

---

## Target customer (ICP)

**Primary:** Boutique rental agency — 2 to 10 agents, 20–100 units, managing residential properties in South Africa.

**Secondary:** Self-managing landlord — 1–5 properties, no in-house agent, wants professional tools without professional prices.

See `content/sales/icp.md` for full segment detail.

---

## Category

**AI-native property management** — not property management software with AI bolted on.

The distinction: every core workflow (lease drafting, maintenance triage, supplier matching, tenant communication) has generative AI built into the primary path, not as an optional add-on. Competitors with "AI" features (AppFolio Realm-X) use predictive/workflow automation — Klikk uses Claude API for generative document creation and RAG-powered tenant chat.

---

## Messaging pillars

### 1. Speed and compliance on every lease
"Your last lease took 3–5 days. Ours takes 10 minutes — and it's RHA-compliant."
- AI generates a fully compliant lease from a natural-language description
- Native e-signing under ECTA s13 — legally valid, no DocuSign account required
- Real-time WebSocket tracking — countersigned PDF in the system before you close the tab

### 2. A platform that speaks South African law
"Not adapted from a UK or US product. Built for the Rental Housing Act."
- RHA clause engine — mandatory and optional clauses baked in
- POPIA-compliant data handling, SA-hosted infrastructure
- ECTA s13 e-signatures — the same legal standard as banking in South Africa
- PPRA-compliant rental mandate workflow

### 3. Flat-rate pricing that scales fairly
"One price. Your whole portfolio. No per-unit surprises."
- **Free** (R0/month): up to 5 properties, bring your own LLM key — start today, no card
- **Pro** (R2,500/month flat): up to 50 properties or 100 units — one price regardless of unit count
- **Enterprise** (R4,000/month base): 100+ properties, white-label portals, on-premises or cloud, POPIA dedicated storage, SLA
- **Custom** (POA): unlimited, dedicated development, BI + ETL, custom integrations

Compare: WeConnectU charges R150–200/unit. At 100 units that is R15,000–R20,000/month. Klikk Pro is R2,500 flat. Break-even vs WeConnectU: 17 units.

### 4. Purpose-built apps for every role
"Four apps. One platform. Everyone gets a tool they'll actually use."
- **Agent app** (iOS + Android): field operations, viewings, prospect capture
- **Tenant app** (iOS + Android): AI chat, maintenance reporting, lease access — not a portal link in an email
- **Owner portal**: real-time occupancy, income, maintenance — owners stop calling
- **Supplier portal**: receive jobs, quote, confirm — no login required

## Honest gaps (do not hide these)

The following are not in Klikk v1.0 and should be stated clearly in sales conversations:
- Trust accounting — pair with PayProp in the meantime
- TPN tenant screening (coming v2)
- Vacancy advertising (Property24 / Private Property syndication, coming v2)
- Incoming / outgoing inspections (coming v2)
- Accounting system connectors (Xero, Sage, QuickBooks — API available today; native connectors v2)

Full transparency builds trust. Prospects who discover gaps after signing become churned customers. Those who know the gaps upfront and still sign become loyal customers.
