# Klikk Market Positioning

## One-Liner

AI-powered property management built for South African rental law.

## Taglines

1. **"Property management, intelligently South African."** — broad marketing
2. **"AI-powered property management for South Africa."** — product/technical
3. **"Click. Lease. Manage. Done."** — ads/social

## Core Differentiators

### 1. SA-First, Not SA-Adapted
Built from the ground up for the Rental Housing Act, POPIA, and South African rental practices. Not a US product with a ZAR currency toggle.

### 2. AI-Native, Not AI-Bolted
Claude AI is woven into every workflow — lease generation, document parsing, maintenance triage, clause generation. Not a chatbot slapped onto a legacy system.

### 3. Full Lifecycle Coverage
15 stages from advertising to deposit refund. Competitors cover payments OR management OR screening. Klikk covers the entire journey.

### 4. Four Apps, One Platform
Every participant gets their own tailored experience:
- **Admin** — command centre for agents and property managers
- **Tenant** — mobile app for reporting, chat, lease viewing, signing
- **Owner** — read-only portfolio dashboard
- **Supplier** — job notifications, quoting, calendar management

### 5. Built by a Landlord
The founder manages their own properties. Every feature solves a real problem, not a hypothetical one.

## Market Position

Klikk sits between:
- **PayProp** (payments only — Klikk manages everything before and after payment)
- **WeConnectU** (enterprise, expensive — Klikk is affordable for small portfolios)
- **Spreadsheets/WhatsApp** (manual chaos — Klikk automates it all)
