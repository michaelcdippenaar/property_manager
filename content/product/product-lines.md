# Klikk Product Lines

Three products, one platform. Klikk serves the full property management and sales lifecycle for South African residential real estate.

---

## 1. Klikk Rentals

**Tagline:** 15 stages, one platform
**Target user:** Rental agents, property managers, landlords
**Scope:** End-to-end residential rental lifecycle — from advertising a vacancy through tenant onboarding, rent collection, maintenance, lease renewal, and move-out.

**Lifecycle:** 15 steps (Advertise → Viewing → Screen → Contract → Sign → Onboard → Deposit → Inspect → Live → Pay Rent → Maintain → Renew → Notice → Out-Inspect → Refund)

**V1 Release scope:**
- Contract signing (AI lease generation, template editor, clause library, document intelligence, native e-signing)
- Maintenance (AI triage, supplier matching, real-time chat)

---

## 2. Klikk Real Estate

**Tagline:** Selling made simple
**Target user:** Estate agents, property practitioners, sellers, conveyancers
**Scope:** Full residential property sales lifecycle — from prospecting sellers through valuation, mandate, marketing, viewings, OTP negotiation, bond approval, legal compliance, and Deeds Office transfer.

**Lifecycle:** 11 steps (Prospect → Valuate → Sign Mandate → List & Market → Viewings → Negotiate → Sign OTP → Buyer Documents → Bond Approval → Legal & Compliance → Transfer)

**Key SA legislation:** Property Practitioners Act 22 of 2019, Alienation of Land Act 68 of 1981, FICA, NCA, CPA

---

## 3. Klikk BI

**Tagline:** Data-driven property decisions
**Target user:** Estate agency principals, team leaders, franchise owners
**Scope:** Business intelligence for estate agents combining market analytics with agency operations tracking.

**Market Analytics:**
- Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) tools
- Area price trends and comparable sales data
- Market reports and property value forecasts
- Suburb-level supply/demand indicators

**Agency Operations:**
- Agent performance dashboards (listings, sales, commission)
- Pipeline tracking (leads → viewings → offers → transfers)
- Commission forecasting and revenue analytics
- Lead source tracking and conversion metrics
- Team KPIs and leaderboards
