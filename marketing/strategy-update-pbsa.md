# Strategy Update — PBSA Market Added as Tier 4

_Added: 2026-04-23. Supplements existing ICP (marketing/sales/icp.md) and launch plan (marketing/campaigns/launch-plan.md)._

## TL;DR

Klikk now runs **two parallel GTM tracks**:

| Track | ICPs | Channel | Voice | Pricing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **SME Rentals** (existing) | T1 boutique PM, T2 landlord, T3 agency | Google Search, LinkedIn, content SEO | Friendly, founder-led | Free → R2,500 → R4,000 |
| **PBSA Operators** (new) | T4 student block owners/operators | LinkedIn, referrals, industry events | Enterprise, operational, metric-driven | R8,000–R40,000+ flat |

Same product, same core features, different messaging.

## How this changes the budget

Current plan: R5k–R10k/month, 70% Google Ads, 20% LinkedIn, 10% content production.

Recommended split after adding PBSA:
- **60% SME funnel** (Google Ads, SEO content, landlord LinkedIn) — R3k–R6k
- **20% PBSA outbound** (LinkedIn Premium, industry event tickets, targeted PBSA content production) — R1k–R2k
- **20% shared brand** (Higgsfield, design, website improvements) — R1k–R2k

PBSA has zero Google Ads spend — operators don't search for software.

## How this changes the roadmap priority

Two features move up if PBSA becomes a real track:
1. **Bulk lease generation UI** — generate 50+ leases from a CSV or previous year's tenancy list
2. **NSFAS invoicing flag** on invoices — direct-to-funder billing with reference numbers

Flag for product: if PBSA sales cycle shows these are deal-blockers, slot them into Q3.

## The 30-day PBSA experiment

Before committing serious effort, run a cheap 4-week test:

1. **Week 1:** Publish PBSA persona on LinkedIn, write 1 operations-angle post
2. **Week 2:** Cold-DM 10 Ops Directors at CampusKey, Respublica, Thrive, Yale Village with the checklist PDF (not demo ask)
3. **Week 3:** Ship the 1-pager PDF and a 5-min Loom showing 50-lease bulk generation
4. **Week 4:** Send Loom to anyone who replied; track demo bookings

**Success bar:** 1 demo booked with a real operator = PBSA track is worth the ongoing investment. Zero = park it, revisit in 6 months.
