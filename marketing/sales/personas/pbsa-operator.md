# Persona — PBSA Operator (Purpose-Built Student Accommodation)

_Added: 2026-04-23. New Tier 4 ICP track. Runs parallel to SME landlord (T1–T3) positioning, not in place of._

---

## Who they are

Operators who own, lease, or manage purpose-built student accommodation — single blocks or portfolios of blocks housing university students. In SA the market is ~R25bn, concentrated in Stellenbosch, Pretoria, Bloemfontein, Potchefstroom, Grahamstown, and Johannesburg.

## Firmographics

| Dimension | Profile |
|---|---|
| Entity type | (Pty) Ltd, sometimes REIT-backed, occasionally university-partnered JV |
| Staff | 5–80 people (building managers, maintenance, admin, tenant services) |
| Portfolio size | **1 block (50–100 beds)** for boutique operators, **10–20 blocks (5 000–20 000 beds)** for scale operators |
| Geography | Tied to university towns — Stellenbosch, Hatfield (Tuks), Kovsies, Tukkies, Wits/UJ, Rhodes |
| Buyer | Operations Director / Asset Manager / CFO (not the building manager) |
| Sales cycle | 3–8 weeks — requires demo, trial with one block, contract negotiation |

## Buying triggers

1. **January intake chaos** — block operator scrambled through Jan 2026 on spreadsheets, vowed never again
2. **New block acquisition** — existing systems don't scale to 2× beds
3. **University partnership tender** — needs professional digital workflow to win the RFP
4. **Existing PMS contract expiring** (WeConnectU, MRI, StarRez for the bigger players)
5. **Board/investor pressure** for better operational visibility and reporting

## Disqualifiers

- Hotel / short-stay operators (Airbnb-style) — different product
- Single-room boarding houses without a central operator
- Universities themselves — procurement cycles too slow for v1

## Pricing positioning

Standard Enterprise at R4,000/month **does not work** at this scale. Propose:

- **PBSA Starter:** R8,000–R12,000/month flat for 1 block (up to 300 beds)
- **PBSA Growth:** R25,000–R40,000/month for up to 10 blocks
- **PBSA Enterprise:** Negotiated, annual contracts, possible per-bed rate

Rationale: StarRez and similar international PMS charge $3–8 per bed per month. At 300 beds that's $1 800–4 800/month = R33 000–R88 000. Klikk undercuts meaningfully while adding SA-specific compliance.

## Channels (PBSA-specific — different from SME)

- **LinkedIn** — primary. Operations Directors are active there.
- **SAASPA conferences** — SA Association of Student Affairs / Private Accommodation, annual event
- **Direct warm intro** — SA PropTech community is small, 2–3 intros unlocks most operator CEOs
- **NSFAS accommodation accreditation lists** — public register of accredited providers = outbound target list
- **Industry press** — Property Wheel, Bizcommunity Property, Property Professional
- **NOT paid search** — operators don't Google for software, they get referred or RFP'd
