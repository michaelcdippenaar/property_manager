## Usage notes

- Keep DMs to two messages maximum before waiting for inbound interest.
- Do not send unsolicited DMs to 1st-degree connections who have never engaged with Klikk content — connect first, wait for acceptance.
- POPIA note: LinkedIn connection + DM involves personal information (name, employer, role). Use only what is publicly shared on their LinkedIn profile. Do not cross-reference with other databases or scraped data.
- Log all LinkedIn outreach in the same CRM as email so contact frequency is controlled across channels.
- If they reply negatively or ask not to be contacted, stop all outreach immediately and log in CRM.
