## FAQ

**Q: Is the free plan really free?**
A: Yes. Manage up to 5 properties with 1 user and 2 AI-generated contracts — completely free. No credit card, no expiry.

**Q: Who is the free plan for?**
A: Owner-managed properties. If you're a landlord managing your own portfolio without an agent, the free plan gives you the essentials: property tracking, AI maintenance reporting, and an owner portal — with your own LLM key.

**Q: What does "bring your own LLM key" mean?**
A: On the free plan, you connect your own Claude or OpenAI API key. Klikk never sees or stores it — your data stays yours. Pro and above include managed LLM access so you don't have to think about it.

**Q: What's the difference between free and Pro?**
A: Free is limited to 5 properties, 2 AI contracts, and requires your own LLM key. Pro unlocks up to 50 properties or 100 units, unlimited AI leases, full e-signing (ECTA s13), maintenance workflows, AI tenant chat, all portals, and REST API + MCP server access.

**Q: How does per-organisation pricing work?**
A: One organisation, one price — R2,500/month for Pro, regardless of how many properties you have (up to 50 properties or 100 units). No per-unit fees.

**Q: Can I add more users?**
A: Yes, on Pro and above. Each plan includes 1 user. Add team members by role — Viewer (R50/month), Agent (R90/month), or Admin (R150/month). Each role has a specific access level and you only pay for what you need.

**Q: What roles can I add?**
A: Viewer (read-only portfolio access), Agent (full access — leases, tenants, applications, inspections), and Admin (full platform access including billing and settings).

**Q: Is there an annual discount?**
A: Yes — 15% off when you pay annually on the Pro plan.

**Q: What's included in Enterprise?**
A: Everything in Pro, plus support for 100+ properties (R50/property above 100), white-label client-facing portals and tenant app, white-label internal admin dashboard, on-premises or cloud hosting, POPIA-compliant dedicated data storage, a dedicated account manager, and SLA guarantee.

**Q: What is the Custom plan?**
A: A fully scoped engagement for organisations that need more than off-the-shelf. Custom includes everything in Enterprise plus custom system integrations (ERP, CRM, legacy platforms), a Business Intelligence layer with custom KPI dashboards, ETL pipelines to your data warehouse, a dedicated development team, and full source code escrow if required. Priced on application.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit card, debit card, and EFT for annual plans.

**Q: Can I migrate from my current system?**
A: Yes. Pro includes a CSV importer for properties, tenants, and leases, plus assisted migration support.

**Q: Is Klikk POPIA compliant?**
A: Yes. All data is hosted in South Africa, encrypted at rest and in transit, and processed in compliance with POPIA requirements. Enterprise and Custom plans include a dedicated isolated data environment with full audit trail.
