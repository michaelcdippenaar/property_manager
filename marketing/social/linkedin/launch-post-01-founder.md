## Post copy

I'm a landlord. I built Klikk because I was tired of my own admin.

Every new tenant took me 2–3 hours:
→ Dig up the Word template from 2019
→ Find-and-replace the names, the rent, the deposit
→ Remember which clauses I updated last time
→ Email it, wait, email again
→ Print, sign, scan, email back

Most SA landlords I know do the same. Some pay an agent 8–10% to do it for them. Neither is good.

So I built the thing I wanted:

✅ Answer 8 questions → RHA-compliant lease generated
✅ Tenant signs on their phone (no printing)
✅ Deposit, inspection, renewal — all tracked in one place
✅ Free for your first 5 properties. Forever.

It's live at klikk.co.za.

If you manage 1–50 rentals and want the SA Rental Agreement Checklist I use (the 12 things your lease must include under RHA s5(2)) — comment "CHECKLIST" and I'll DM it.

No funnel. No credit card. Just the PDF.
