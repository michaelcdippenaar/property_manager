## What this study involves

You are being invited to take part in a usability test of the Klikk property management software. The session takes about 30 minutes. You will be asked to complete a series of tasks in the app while thinking aloud. You will also complete a short questionnaire at the end.

There are no right or wrong answers. We are testing the software, not you.

---

## What data we collect

| Type | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Screen recording (mouse movement, clicks, scroll) | Identify usability problems | Deleted within 60 days of session |
| Audio recording (think-aloud comments) | Understand your reasoning | Deleted within 60 days of session |
| Questionnaire responses | Measure satisfaction score | Retained in anonymised form |
| Any test data you enter into the app | Required to run the scenarios | Deleted from the staging environment within 7 days |

**Sensitive fields are masked.** Password fields, ID numbers, and bank detail fields are automatically hidden in the screen recording. You will not be asked to enter your real personal or financial details — use fictional test data.

---

## Your rights under POPIA

This study is conducted in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

- **Participation is voluntary.** You may stop at any time, for any reason, without consequence.
- **You may withdraw consent** after the session by emailing mc@tremly.co.za. Your data will be deleted within 5 business days.
- **You may request access** to the data we hold about you.
- **Your identity is not linked** to session recordings. We use a tester code, not your name.

For questions about how your data is handled, contact our Information Officer at mc@tremly.co.za.

## In-app consent banner

When you first open the staging app, a banner will appear asking whether you allow session recording. Please click **"Allow recording"** before starting the tasks. This is separate from this paper consent — both are required.
