Klikk Agent is the mobile command centre for South African property rental agents.

Run your full rental pipeline from your phone — book viewings, capture prospects, create and send leases, track maintenance requests, and monitor your portfolio at a glance.

KEY FEATURES

Viewing management
• Book and manage viewings with built-in calendar
• Capture prospect details on-site with a tap
• Convert a viewing into a lease in seconds

Leases
• Create legally compliant SA rental agreements
• Send for e-signature without printing a single page
• Track signing status in real time

Maintenance
• Log and assign maintenance requests
• Co-ordinate suppliers with location-aware dispatch
• Photo attachments for before/after documentation

Pipeline & Dashboard
• Full rental cycle visibility in one screen
• Property portfolio at a glance
• Today-view for your daily action list

People
• Tenant, owner, and supplier contact book
• Search across all your managed properties

DESIGNED FOR SOUTH AFRICAN RENTALS

Klikk is built on the Rental Housing Act and aligned with POPIA data-privacy requirements. All data is stored securely on South African servers.

REQUIREMENTS

• Active Klikk Agent subscription (contact your agency or sign up at klikk.co.za)
• Android 6.0 (API 23) or later
• Internet connection required
